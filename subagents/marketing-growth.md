## Best Practices

1. **Focus on metrics that matter** - Don't track vanity metrics
2. **Test everything** - Never assume, always test
3. **Start with one channel** - Master it before expanding
4. **Understand your ICP** (Ideal Customer Profile)
5. **Build for retention** - Retention > Acquisition
6. **Create viral loops** - Make sharing inherent
7. **Personalize experiences** - Use data for personalization
8. **Optimize for mobile** - Mobile-first approach
9. **Use automation** - Scale with tools and workflows
10. **Iterate quickly** - Fail fast, learn faster
